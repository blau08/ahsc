Asian Health & Service Center - Social Media Dashboard

Analyzing and improving digital engagement through data-driven insights

Project Overview

This project focused on gathering and analyzing insights from the **organization's digital assets**, including Google Analytics, Facebook Insights, LinkedIn, Google Maps, and their website. The goal was to understand visitor trends, demographics, and engagement patterns to make data-driven decisions.

Key Insights & Analysis

  • Tracked website traffic and user engagement using Google Analytics.
  • Analyzed **year-over-year growth** of social media presence.
  • Created **pie charts, line graphs, and trend analysis reports** for better visualization.
  • Identified top-performing content and user demographics.

Technologies & Platforms Used

As part of the project, I introduced several new technologies to the organization:

  • **Google Analytics** – Set up tracking and created accounts for real-time insights.
  • **Website Migration** – Moved the old website to a new WordPress platform.
  • **Slack for Nonprofits** – Implemented Slack for team communication.
  • **Google Forms Automation** – Designed a facility rental application process.

Impact & Results

  • **Increased engagement** on social media and website traffic.
  • More efficient **facility rental process** with Google Forms automation.
  • Strengthened **corporate partnerships** with Nike & Columbia Sportswear.
